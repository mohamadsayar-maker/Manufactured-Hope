# Manufactured-Hope
Manufactured Hope is an interactive experience inspired by the Iranian game Gol Ya Pooch. Participants choose between the left &amp; the right hand, searching for hope, truth, or a way forward. But each reveal is empty.
The work reflects the political condition where both Left and Right have failed the public, turning choice into performance and hope into something manufactured
I, repeated, and
withdrawn. It explores the psychology of living through political instability and post-war uncertainty, where the act of choosing remains constant, even when the choices themselves become illusions.
